About PeerLearn

A platform connecting MMU IT diploma students with peer tutors to create effective learning experiences and build a collaborative academic community.

Our Mission

Who We Are

At Peerlearn, we're dedicated to creating a vibrant peer-to-peer learning ecosystem within the Multimedia University community. Our mission is to connect students seeking academic support with knowledgeable peers who excel in specific subjects, fostering collaborative learning and academic excellence.

Peerlearn is a student-centered platform designed specifically for MMU IT diploma students. We believe that some of the best learning happens when students teach and learn from each other. Our platform harnesses the diverse knowledge and skills within our student body to create meaningful educational connections.

What We Offer

Peer-to-Peer Learning

Connect with fellow students who excel in subjects you find challenging

Flexible Scheduling

Book sessions that fit your busy academic schedule

Subject Variety

Find tutors across a wide range of MMU courses and programs

Verified Tutors

All tutors are MMU students with verified academic credentials

Community Platform

Engage in forums, group discussions, and collaborative study events

Our Goals

1

Facilitate Peer Learning

We're building a community of 100+ students and facilitating 50+ peer-to-peer learning sessions in our first five months.

2

Improve Academic Performance

Our tutoring sessions are designed to boost understanding and confidence, with 80% of regular participants reporting improved academic performance.

3

Enhance Learning Experience

Our user-friendly platform makes it easy to find the right tutor, book sessions, and provide feedback, maintaining a 90% user satisfaction rate.

4

Build A Community

Beyond individual sessions, we're creating spaces for weekly discussions, resource sharing, and monthly peer-led study events.

Who Can Benefit

Students Seeking Help

Undergraduate and postgraduate MMU IT diploma students looking for academic assistance in challenging subjects. Our platform helps you find the perfect peer tutor to address your specific learning needs.

Tutors

High-achieving students who want to reinforce their knowledge, gain teaching experience, and earn extra income by helping peers. Share your expertise and build valuable skills for your future career.
