Frequently Asked Questions

What qualifications do I need to become a tutor?
To become a tutor on PeerLearn, you should have a strong academic record in the subjects you wish to teach (typically a B+ or higher). You'll need to provide transcripts or other evidence of your academic performance. Prior tutoring experience is beneficial but not required.
How much can I earn as a tutor?
Earnings vary based on your subject expertise, experience, and how many hours you choose to tutor. Most tutors charge between RM15-30 per hour. Top tutors with excellent reviews and specialized knowledge can command higher rates. You set your own rates on our platform.
How do I receive payment?
Tutoring fees are settled directly between students and tutors. PeerLearn is not responsible for any disputes arising from these transactions.
How many hours can I tutor per week?
You have complete flexibility to determine your tutoring hours. Some tutors work just a few hours per week, while others choose to tutor 10-15 hours or more. You can adjust your availability at any time through coordination with the request session.
